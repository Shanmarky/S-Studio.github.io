S-DANCE STUDIO CENTER

EAT. SLEEP. DANCE. REPEAT.

This Studio is not just a place of learning dance, this is a lively and open space for the ones of different ages and different levels who come to visit the art of movement.
In here you'll learn everything and learn how to perform most of the different varieties of ballet including classical ballet, Hip hop, contemporay, Afro, Femme, and more!
Our experienced instructors with a focus on the basics and your personal growth constantly offer you a hand both to build up your confidence and flourish your creativity.

OUR COMMUNITY

The choreography and rhythm are but part of S-Studio Dance Center community not only a second family,
but drawing together for a shared passion and an expression creating long lasting friendships.
It's a place where you soak into others, acknowledge your evolution, one another and find your voice through dance.

DANCE WITH PASSION

So dance with passion and love his knowledge with a enthusiasm and the musical beat will carry you higher. Leap with courage, learn ritually, to dance with the music upwards.
At S-Studio Dance Center you are not getting taught by someone but trying to learn to know yourself through every movement.
